## Sans
[Noto Sans](https://www.google.com/get/noto/)
Noto is a font developed by Google. With it's multiple styles and weights and support for pretty much everything you could need, it's always a solid choice. The name noto is to convey the idea that Google’s goal is to see “**no** more **to**fu” (TOFU EXAMPLE).
